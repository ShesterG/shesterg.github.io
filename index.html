Research

In a nutshell, I am interested in what language truly means beyond the speech and text modalities. To have a grasp of this, I am keen on how to make computers understand the whole visual languages spectrum which can be reflected by the Kendon's Continuum. As a case example, I have been working on Automatic Signed Languages Processing, with interest on anything rotating around that as Multi(and Cross)modality, (Low Resource) Machine Translation and Long/Dense Videos Understanding.
